Our community values

CC² exists to help people build genuine friendships — safely, respectfully, and without pressure.

  • Be kind, curious, and considerate
  • Respect boundaries — no means no
  • Friendship only (no dating or sexual content)
  • Everyone deserves to feel safe here

What’s not allowed

  • Harassment, bullying, or intimidation
  • Sexual messages or unwanted advances
  • Hate speech or discrimination
  • Sharing personal contact details too quickly
  • Pressuring someone to meet offline

Reporting & blocking

If something doesn’t feel right, trust that feeling.

  • You can report a profile, message, or comment
  • Reports are reviewed discreetly
  • Accounts may be warned, limited, or removed

If you’re feeling unsafe

CC² is not a crisis service. If you’re in immediate danger or distress, please reach out to local support:

  • Ireland: Samaritans 116 123
  • UK: Samaritans 116 123
  • Emergency: 999 / 112
