Safety & Community

Our community promise

CC² exists to help people build genuine friendships. This is a non-dating, non-sexual space designed around kindness, respect, and shared interests.

What’s encouraged

  • Friendly conversation
  • Respectful curiosity
  • Inclusive language
  • Clear boundaries
  • Community-minded behaviour

What’s not allowed

  • Sexual or romantic advances
  • Harassment or intimidation
  • Hate speech or exclusion
  • Persistent unwanted contact
  • Sharing personal contact details publicly

Reporting & moderation

If something doesn’t feel right, you can report it discreetly. Reports are reviewed calmly and fairly.

We prioritise education first, then boundaries, and only escalate when needed.
